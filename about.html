  • How did they come into your possession?

    My first doll was from a garage sale. I was 9 years old and young and full of life. I still remember the lady who sold it to me, her hair was a pure white and very long (quite majestic) and she said to me “keep her safe” and I have till this day. Usually I obtain my dolls from Bazares (mexican antique stores) or thrift stores, anywhere really.

  • Why have they remained in your possession for the amount of time they have?

    I am a lover of antiques, my grandmother and I splurge on antiques more than we should and porcelin dolls are easy to find. Sadly their creepy reputation prevents them from finding homes but I find them precious and beautiful. Plus they always are dressed so majestically

  • Why do they spark joy?

    They inspire me cause they're fabulous.
